About the app

Manage your Intoxalock device with our easy, time-saving app. The Intoxalock app offers self-service access to all of the important features in your account - anytime, anywhere.

Key Features:
• Track your account status and actions needed to remain in good standing
• Pay and go! Get notices of your upcoming statements and pay in seconds
• Get calibrations reminders and order calibrations using the app
• Get in touch with your service center
• Save all your DUI contacts in one place
• Be able to view, sign and even extend your lease
• Request lockout reset
• Access information on how to best use your interlock device
• Available on iOS and any Android device
